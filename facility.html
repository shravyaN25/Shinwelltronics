Comprehensive wire harness assembly with dedicated foam boards for complex assemblies

Assembly Process Overview

The wire harness assembly process is completed in several steps to ensure wires are grouped, protected, and connected for electrical systems.

Design and Planning

Designers create detailed schematics and layouts based on project requirements, considering component placement, space limitations, environmental conditions, and power needs.

Wire Preparation

Once the design is made, wires are cut to required lengths, and ends are stripped to expose the conductors.

Crimping and Soldering

Technicians attach terminals to wires using crimping tools or soldering, ensuring permanent electrical connections.

Assembly of Connectors

Based on the design layout, terminals are inserted into connectors.

Bundling and Protection

Bundling wires with cable ties, sleeves, and wraps, and implementing protective measures to prevent damage during handling, installation, and operation.
